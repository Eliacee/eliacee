What Inspires Us to Build This Café

Yuriel’s Hideout began as a dream shared between two coffee lovers. As partners in life and now in business, we spent countless afternoons in quiet corners of our favorite cafés, imagining a space of our own. We wanted to create a place that captured that same magic 'hideout' where our passion for premium coffee meets the warmth of Japanese-inspired hospitality. This café is our dream brought to life, and we’re so happy to share it with you
